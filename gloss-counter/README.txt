To move the player use the 'WASD' keys to go up, left, down, right respectivly.
Use the spacebar to shoot bullets.
When in game, press 'P' to pause the game and open the menu.
When in the menu, use 'W' and 'S' keys to go up and down in the menu and press spacebar to confirm your choice

As explained in the design document, the overlay parameter of the world datatype is used for debugging purposes. 
So their are function at the bottom of Controller.hs file that can be activated if needed. Their is a section in controller.hs named Debugging so it is not hard to find.
